# DAX — Lighting System (Light Mode)

# Purpose

This document defines the cinematic lighting system for the DAX light mode experience.

Lighting is one of the most important emotional systems of the website.

The interface should not feel designed with colors alone.

The interface should feel sculpted by light.

Lighting must create:
- atmosphere
- depth
- emotion
- luxury perception
- immersion
- cinematic realism

The website should feel illuminated from within.


---

# Core Lighting Philosophy

Lighting should behave like environmental light.

Not graphic decoration.

The light system should feel:
- soft
- atmospheric
- diffused
- cinematic
- premium
- spatial
- immersive

The interface should feel physically illuminated.


---

# Cinematic Inspiration

The lighting language should feel inspired by:
- futuristic architecture
- luxury product cinematography
- sci-fi environmental lighting
- premium industrial design
- atmospheric photography
- high-end exhibitions

The website should feel lit like a cinematic space.


---

# Light Mode Lighting Identity

The DAX light mode should feel:
- luminous
- airy
- atmospheric
- futuristic
- elegant
- softly radiant

The environment should feel filled with diffused light.

Not flat white brightness.


---

# Environmental Lighting Philosophy

Lighting should exist everywhere subtly.

Use:
- ambient diffusion
- soft glow
- radial lighting
- atmospheric bloom
- edge illumination
- reflected light
- cinematic haze

Avoid:
- obvious spotlight effects
- exaggerated neon
- hard lighting
- artificial glow spam

Light should feel natural and immersive.


---

# Radial Light System

Radial lighting should create:
- depth
- focus
- atmosphere
- emotional softness

Use:
- large blurred radial gradients
- subtle environmental illumination
- cinematic diffusion
- soft visual anchoring

Radial lighting should feel atmospheric.

Not decorative.


---

# Glow Philosophy

Glow should feel:
- premium
- elegant
- restrained
- cinematic

Use:
- soft bloom
- atmospheric diffusion
- reflected luminosity
- environmental glow interaction

Avoid:
- strong neon
- aggressive cyberpunk glow
- overexposed highlights
- cheap luminous effects

Glow should feel emotionally intelligent.


---

# Ambient Illumination

The entire environment should feel softly illuminated.

Use:
- subtle background light
- environmental luminosity
- layered atmospheric brightness
- diffused visual softness

Avoid:
- isolated lighting effects
- empty flat backgrounds
- dead white surfaces

The page should feel alive with light.


---

# Edge Lighting Philosophy

Edge lighting should create:
- dimensionality
- elegance

- premium softness

Use:
- subtle edge highlights
- atmospheric reflections

Edges should feel softly illuminated.


---

# Surface Lighting

Surfaces should react to environmental lighting.

Use:
- soft reflections
- subtle luminosity
- cinematic gradients
- atmospheric transparency
- reflected glow

Surfaces should feel physically present.


---

# Lighting and Depth

Lighting should reinforce:
- spatial hierarchy
- atmospheric layering
- dimensional composition
- environmental realism

Use:
- blurred illumination
- depth haze
- foreground/background contrast
- cinematic separation

Light should help define space.


---

# White Lighting Philosophy

White should glow softly.

Avoid:
- flat white emptiness
- sterile minimalism
- hard UI brightness

Use:
- pearl illumination
- cinematic softness
- atmospheric diffusion
- environmental variation

Whites should feel luminous and premium.


---

# Reflection Philosophy

Subtle reflections should reinforce luxury perception.

Use:
- soft metallic reflections
- atmospheric surface response
- blurred luminosity
- cinematic shine

Avoid:
- glossy fake reflections
- aggressive chrome aesthetics
- exaggerated shine

Reflections should feel restrained and elegant.


---

# Blur and Light Interaction

Blur should interact with light naturally.

Use:
- glow diffusion
- atmospheric softness
- environmental haze
- cinematic bloom
- light scattering

Blur helps light feel physical.


---

# Typography Lighting

Typography should interact with environmental light.

Use:
- subtle luminosity
- soft atmospheric contrast
- elegant highlight response

Avoid:
- harsh text contrast
- overly dark typography
- flat readability systems

Text should feel integrated into the lighting environment.


---

# Image Lighting Integration

Images must share:
- environmental lighting
- glow direction

---

# Background Lighting System

Backgrounds should never feel static.

Use:
- atmospheric gradients
- subtle light movement
- environmental diffusion
- layered illumination

The background should feel spatial and alive.


---

# Motion Lighting

Lighting can contain subtle movement.

Use:
- slow ambient shifts
- glow breathing
- environmental drift
- atmospheric transitions

Avoid:
- flashing
- pulsing effects
- dramatic animated glow

Movement should remain nearly subconscious.


---

# Hero Lighting Philosophy

Use:
- cinematic glow
- layered luminosity
- atmospheric focus lighting
- environmental bloom
- soft volumetric diffusion

The first screen should feel emotionally radiant.


---

# Transparency and Light

Transparent elements should react to light.

Use:
- blurred luminosity

---

Luxury lighting is created through:
- restraint
- softness
